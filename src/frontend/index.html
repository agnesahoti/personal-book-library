About This App

Built for book lovers,
by book lovers.

Personal Library is a simple, elegant tool to keep track of every book you own, have read, or plan to read. No accounts, no clutter — just you and your books.

  • ✦ Add books with title, author and genre
  • ✦ Search and filter your collection instantly
  • ✦ Edit or remove books at any time
  • ✦ Tracks your total books and authors
